Ham and Beans

Description

A quick and easy meal with minimal working time and a good use of leftover ham. The general steps are as follows:

This meal involves rinsing beans and leaving them soaking overnight. Following that the beans ham and other flavor additives are kept in a hotpot for 1-2 hours until completion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry great Northern beans
  • 1/2pound cooked ham, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1/4teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 6 cups water
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse and transfer beans to a hotpot. Soak in cold water for at least 8 hours. Draind and rinse the beans.
  2. Add beans back to hotpot alongside ham, onion, sugar, parsley, and cayenne pepper. Pour in enough water to cover and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil: reduce heat to low and simmer until beans are tender for 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Add more water if needed.
