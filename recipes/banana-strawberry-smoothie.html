Banana Strawberry Smoothie

Description

A nice quick and easy meal to get in your daily fruit count

This drink uses bananas and smoothies and is surprisingly filling. It can fit the role of a snack or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • 3/4cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon banana extract
  • 1 (8 oz) container strawberry yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Place banana, banana extract, milk, yogurt, and sugar into a blender. Blend until smooth and serve.
