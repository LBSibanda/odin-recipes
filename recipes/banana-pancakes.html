Banana Pancakes

Description

One of the best breakfast foods combining two of my favourite things: bananas and pancakes.

This meal is quick and will take less then 20 minuts to make for a banging breaksfast.Everything you need will be easily found in a kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas in a second bowl.
  2. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture:batter will be slightly lumpy
  3. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour batter onto the pan using approximately 1/4cup for eahc pancake. Cook until pancakes are golden brown, 3-5 minutes per side.
